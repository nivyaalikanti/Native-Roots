Mumbai

Description

Mumbai, the city of dreams, is famous for its vibrant nightlife, historic landmarks, and bustling markets. Explore the Gateway of India, stroll along Marine Drive, and visit the Elephanta Caves.

Best Season to Visit

The best time to visit Mumbai is from November to February when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing.

Local Food

Enjoy Mumbai's street food like Vada Pav, Pav Bhaji, and Bhel Puri. For a fine dining experience, try the local seafood and Indian cuisine at renowned restaurants.

Budget

Estimated Budget: INR 3000-5000 per person per day (including accommodation, food, and local travel).

Modes of Transport

Local transport options include taxis, auto-rickshaws, and the Mumbai local trains. For longer distances, you can use buses or hire a private car.
